COVID-19

Corona viruses are a large group of viruses that may cause disease in animals and humans.
It is known that a number of corona viruses cause human cases of respiratory infections
that range from common colds to more severe diseases such as Middle Eastern
Respiratory Syndrome and severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS).
The newly discovered Coronavirus causes the Corvid-19 virus.
